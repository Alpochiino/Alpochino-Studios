About Me

Hello there! My name is Christian, i am a passionate individual from Sweden embarking on the exciting journey of creating my first ever indie game. Armed with creativity and a dream, I'm dedicated to bringing unique and captivating gaming experiences to life for the beloved gaming community. This is a dream come true to work on my game. Ever since i was a little child i have dreamt of making a game myself and now that dream could be true.

As a solo developer, I aim to craft a game that not only entertains but also reflects my unique vision and storytelling. Every game i make is a game that belongs to my heart, I aspire to establish my own gaming studio, contributing to the ever-evolving world of interactive entertainment. Join me on this adventure, and let's explore the boundless possibilities of game development together!
